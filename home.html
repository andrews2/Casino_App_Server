Wolfpack Casino

Welcome to Wolfpack casino. This app was made as a part of of a senior design project by ECE students at North Carolina State University. The mobile application can be used to scan in casino chips, which were also part of the project, and store them in a digital wallet. This allows users to secure thier chips and better keep track of which chips are theirs. Using NFC technology chips can be added to any users digital wallet with the simple tap of their phone. RFID is used as well to allow the casino to constantly locate chips. Please download our Android app below, sign up for an account and test out our senior design project.
